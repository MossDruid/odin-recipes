Hardtack

Description

Ancient, simple survival bread. Has long self life. Easy to make. Recommended to soak before eating.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix 2 cups flour, .75 cups water and 3 tsp salt to create dough.
  2. Roll dough out to .25 inch or .5 inch thickness.
  3. Cut into squares and poke holes in the dough.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 30 minutes, flip and bake for another 20-30 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and store in dry area after cooling.
