Tea

Description

A simple pleasant drink that is easy to prepare and has been drunk all over the world for millennia.

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat water to boiling point or near boiling.
  2. Add tea leaves either free floating or in some type of strainer to water.
  3. 'Steep' tea for 2-5 minutes. This means to let the leaves soak in the water.
  4. Optionally extra plant ingredients may be added with tea leaves for additional flavor.
  5. Remove leaves and drink.
